    Be the Present Simple Tense

    ##Be(am/is/are) Usage: BE

    be + noun be + adjective be + preposition

    Grammar: BE #

    Subject + BE

    I + am You/We/They + are He/She/It + is ##Examples

    Subject BE
    I am teacher.
    You are beautiful.
    He is at work.
    She is in London.
    It is a book.
    We are in the library.
    They are busy.
